NCSU IEEE Robotics Team

About the robotics team:

The IEEE Robotics Team is an entirely NC State student run organization, fueled by the passion of individuals to do cool stuff. We are an interdisciplinary group made up primarily of Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science students; but all are welcome. From Maker-Masters to Techie-Tenderfoots, as long as you have an interest in learning you can contribute to the team.


Meetings:

The robotics team meets on Tuesdays at 6:30 in EB2 1003
